# Rancor Media Bot — Admin Operational Guide

Welcome, Admin! This document simplifies managing your referral bot. Everything is controlled directly from your Telegram chat with the bot.

---

## 1. Monitoring New Users
When a new user joins, you will receive an automatic notification. To see everyone waiting for your manual approval:
*   **Command:** `/unverified`
*   **What it does:** Displays a list of all registered users who are waiting for you to verify them.

## 2. Verifying Users (Manual Approval)
After a user has joined your WhatsApp group and sent you the screenshot of your saved contact, you must activate their account so they can start referring others and earning.
*   **Command:** `/verify <User_Telegram_ID>`
*   **Example:** `/verify 8204006525`
*   **What happens:** 
    1. The user's account is activated.
    2. The person who referred them is automatically paid their reward.
    3. Both the referrer and the new user are notified.

## 3. Managing Payouts
When a user hits 3 verified referrals and requests a payout:
*   **Command:** `/payouts`
*   **What it does:** Shows all pending requests, including the user's name, WhatsApp number, amount, and bank details.
*   **To Finish Payout:** After sending the money to the user's bank account, type:
    *   `/approve <Request_ID>`
*   **What happens:** The user is notified that their payout has been processed.

## 4. Admin Directory (The User List)
To see a full list of all your users, their referral counts, and their earnings:
*   **Command:** `/listusers`
*   **What it shows:** 
    *   `✅` (Verified) or `⏳` (Unverified)
    *   User Name & ID
    *   WhatsApp Number
    *   Referral Count
    *   Earnings (₦)

## 5. Security & Settings
*   **Ban a User:** If you find a cheater, type `/ban <User_Telegram_ID>`.
*   **Unban a User:** Type `/unban <User_Telegram_ID>`.
*   **Update Rewards:** Change the referral reward amount (e.g., to ₦200) by typing `/setreward 200`.
*   **Update WhatsApp Links:** 
    *   To update your group: `/setlink group <New_URL>`
    *   To update your contact link: `/setlink contact <New_URL>`

---

## Quick Admin Checklist
- [ ] **Check notifications:** When you get a "New User Pending" alert, check their WhatsApp for the screenshot.
- [ ] **Verify:** Run `/verify <ID>` once confirmed.
- [ ] **Payouts:** Check `/payouts` daily and run `/approve <ID>` after payment.
- [ ] **Security:** Use `/listusers` to check for suspicious activity.

*If you need help, simply type `/admin` to the bot to see this command list at any time.*
